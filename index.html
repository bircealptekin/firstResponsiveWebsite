Why vegetarian?

Everyone has their own reasons, some people care bout animal rights, some people care about the environment, some people care about their own health and some people care about all of them. You might just want to try it without any reason as well. If you want to learn more about vegetarianism we will link a few resources that might be interesting to check out:

  • Vegetarian Society of the United Kingdom

  • Forks Over Knives
